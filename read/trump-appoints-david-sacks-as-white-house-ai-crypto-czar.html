Trump Appoints David Sacks as White House AI & Crypto Czar

Trump appoints a SOL bull as his chief advisor on AI and Crypto.

President-elect Donald Trump has appointed David O. Sacks, a tech entrepreneur and pro-crypto advocate, as the White House's new "A.I. and Crypto Czar." The appointment highlights the incoming administrations focus on advancing cryptocurrency regulation and artificial intelligence leadership in the U.S.

Whats the Scoop?

  • Sacks will lead A.I. and crypto efforts while chairing the Presidential Council of Advisors for Science and Technology
  • Sacks goals are to develop a legal framework for clearer U.S. cryptocurrency regulations
  • Sacks tech background includes founding Yammer and serving as COO of PayPal

Bankless Take

This appointment underscores Trumps strategic pivot toward crypto and A.I. leadership, signaling a more favorable regulatory stance. Sacks tech-savvy credentials suggest an effort to bridge the gap between innovation and policy, but balancing growth with guardrails will be a critical challenge. With Sacks and Atkins onboard, the crypto industry could see meaningful regulatory clarity, potentially reversing the U.S.'s lag in global competitiveness.
